bayou
A private AI agent that runs entirely on your own Mac — your model, your files, your chats never leave the machine. Free to use. Requires Apple Silicon (M-series).
1
Install the helper — paste this into Terminal:
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/epsteinj/bayou-desktop/main/helper/install.sh | bash
Want to read it first? Inspect the script ↗
2
That's it — you're done.
The helper auto-starts on login and listens only on localhost. This page connects on its own the moment it's up. First run, it'll offer to download a model (just once).
